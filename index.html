The Start

We started this app in order to make a difference in the world. We want to help people find their career path and make the right choices. This was started as a school project for BCIT.

Our goal

Our goal is to help people find their career path and make the right choices. We want to make a difference in the world and help people achieve their dreams. We know its hard to choose a career path, especially for young people, hard to know what youre getting yourself into

How we'll do it

We will achieve this by getting you to do a personalisation survey to best match you with a career. You will be bale to check the latest news of your selected career as well as talk to profesionals in the field and see what companies are looking for.
